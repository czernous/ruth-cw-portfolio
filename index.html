  • About

    Life story

    I was born on the 5th of February 1989 in Lagos, Nigeria. I moved to England when I was a kid with my mum to join our family. In England I went to boarding school where I got the opportunity to hone my creative skills.

    I went to Drama classes for 5 years, attended speech and drama competitions and also took LAMDA exams. I was also involved in a creative writing competition where I got my poem published alongside other students at my school.

    At 15 I completed my GCSE’s one of which was Art and Design and went on to study an AS and a Foundation Diploma in Art and Design. Following that, I completed a BA Hons in Textile Design and Fine Art at Bath Spa University. After university I worked for a few years as a care assistant then, started a PGCE to teach Art and Design in London.

    However, things didn’t go to plan. So, I dropped out and then completed a Trinity TESOL and went off to Spain to teach English as a foreign language. I lived in Spain for 8 months, taught and lived in Vietnam for 2 years and then moved to Thailand and lived there for 6 months.

    Copywriting

    My interest in copywriting began as a means to progress in my teaching career. I looked at different options from Director of studies to being a teacher trainer. None of which appealed to me. After speaking to a work colleague about her intentions to be a copywriter.

    I decided to look into it. At this point. I had no idea what copywriting was. But I did remember my obsession with reading novels as a child and being praised for my research skills at university. So, I knew it could be a well suited career.

    I read books to gain more knowledge and signed up on a Copywriting course to improve my writing skills. This course has been a lifesaver as I learned to write using different techniques to create different types of copy; Advertising features, Direct mail, Pitches and more.

    Whilst studying I realized that I needed some experience. So, I did some freelance work for an advertising company writing content for ecommerce websites.

    Today

    I would like to carry on with my professional development by doing some more courses on SEO and Social Media Marketing.

    I have a good knowledge of these areas from doing my own research but would like to take my knowledge to the next level by embarking on a course. Also, I aim to find a company where I can utilise the skills I have learnt and continue to progress within this field.
